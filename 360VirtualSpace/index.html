Your Virtual Office 

As we continue to work virtually, are you looking for tips on how to set yourself up for success? You're in the right place! With this interactive experience, you'll get some quick and easy-to-implement actions to update your workspace.

INSTRUCTIONS
Select the "Get Started' button, and then press "( ▶ )" to view the 360 virtual office. Once you're in, you navigate by clicking on an area, holding the click, and moving to the left and right to see different parts of the room. Select the "i" markers to read best practices for working virtually.
