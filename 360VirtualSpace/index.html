Your Virtual Office 

As many Comcast employees continue to work virtually, it allows an opportunity to reflect on how you work and think of ways to refresh your workspace for success going forward. With this interactive experience, you’ll be provided with some easy, actionable steps to get you started!

When you are ready, you may continue by:

① Clicking on the button below that says “Let’s Get Started”
② Hold your left mouse button down and pull the mouse to the left and right to spin the view around each scenario
③ Click on the “i” markers to learn actionable best practices on working virtually
